Brief: with login, support for English, Dutch, Arabic and Putonghua; intial assessment-scenario training and dashboard
Frontline Boost is an AI-powered training simulator designed specifically for hotel frontline staff. It allows you to practice handling challenging guest situations through realistic, voice-based conversations with AI personas.
During your practice, a 'Live Mentor' provides instant tips to guide you. After each session, you receive a detailed performance report, scoring you on five key service pillars: Empathy, Communication, Ownership, Consistency, and Personalization. The goal is to help you build confidence and master the skills needed to provide exceptional guest service in any situation.
How to Use the App
Get Started: Create an account using your email and password. You can select your preferred language (English, Dutch, Arabic, or Chinese) on the login screen.
Your First Task: You'll begin with a one-time Initial Assessment. Click 'Start' and handle the guest scenario by tapping the large microphone button to speak. When you're done, click it again to stop.
Review Your Feedback: After finishing, you'll receive a detailed report on your performance. Review it to understand your strengths and areas for improvement.
Mentored Training: From your Dashboard, you can now start a New Mentored Training. Choose any scenario you'd like to practice.
Practice with a Coach: During these mentored sessions, the Live Mentor panel will appear with helpful tips to guide you as you speak.
Track Your Progress: All your completed sessions and performance data will be available on your Dashboard. Keep practicing to watch your skills grow

## Run Locally

**Prerequisites:**  Node.js


1. Install dependencies:
   `npm install`
2. Set the `GEMINI_API_KEY` in [.env.local](.env.local) to your Gemini API key
3. Run the app:
   `npm run dev`

# Frontline Boost

**Frontline Boost** is an AI-powered training simulator designed specifically for hotel frontline staff. It allows you to practice handling challenging guest situations through realistic, voice-based conversations with **AI personas**.

During your practice, a **Live Mentor** provides instant tips to guide you. After each session, you receive a detailed performance report, scoring you on five key service pillars:

- Empathy  
- Communication  
- Ownership  
- Consistency  
- Personalization  

The goal is to help you build confidence and master the skills needed to provide exceptional guest service in any situation.

---

## How to Use the App

### 1. Get Started
- Create an account using your email and password.  
- Select your preferred language (**English, Dutch, Arabic, or Chinese**) on the login screen.

### 2. Your First Task
- Begin with a one-time **Initial Assessment**.  
- Click **Start** and handle the guest scenario by tapping the large microphone button to speak.  
- When you're done, click it again to stop.

### 3. Review Your Feedback
- After completing the session, you’ll receive a **detailed performance report**.  
- Review your scores to identify your strengths and areas for improvement.

### 4. Mentored Training
- From your **Dashboard**, start a **New Mentored Training** and choose any scenario you’d like to practice.

### 5. Practice with a Coach
- During mentored sessions, the **Live Mentor panel** appears with helpful tips that guide you in real-time.

### 6. Track Your Progress
- All completed sessions and performance data are saved to your **Dashboard**.  
- Keep practicing to watch your skills grow over time.

---

## Key Features

- Voice-based, realistic guest interaction simulation  
- AI mentor providing live coaching tips  
- Performance analytics across five service pillars  
- Multilingual interface (English, Dutch, Arabic, Chinese)  
- Personalized training progress tracking

# Student How‑To Manual (Frontline Boost / Project Tracking)

This manual walks you through your **student UI journey**: what you’ll see on-screen and what to click to submit updates, read feedback, and request purchases.

## What this app is for (student view)

- Track your project progress week-by-week.
- Submit **weekly updates** for instructor review.
- Share links (designs, docs, repos, demos) as evidence.
- Request purchases for your project (your instructor approves/rejects).

## Access and accounts

### Sign in options

1. Open the app.
2. Go to **Sign in**.
3. Use the sign-in method your course uses. Some courses use a **magic link**:
   - Enter your email and click **Email me a magic link (sign up)**.
   - Fill in the **First time here?** section (Full name, optional HKUST email, and Programme).
   - Open the link from your email.
   - If the app asks again, re-enter your email and click **Complete sign-in**.

After signing in, you’ll land on your student dashboard.

## Your dashboard

### “My projects”

After login you’ll see a list of projects you’re assigned to.

- Click a project to open its detail page (weekly updates + purchase requests).
- If you see “You haven't been assigned to any projects yet”, contact your instructor/admin.

## Submit a weekly update

1. Open your project.
2. Click **Submit update**.
3. Fill out the form:
   - **Goals for this week**:
     - Add at least one goal.
     - Mark each goal as **Achieved** or not.
     - If not achieved, add a short reason.
   - **Goals for next week**: what you plan to do next.
   - **Blockers**: anything preventing progress.
   - **Overall project progress (%)**: adjust the slider (0–100).
   - **External documents & links**: add label + URL (designs, PRs, docs, demo videos, etc.).
4. Click **Submit update for review**.

After submission:

- Your update appears with status **Pending** (waiting for instructor review).
- You can still read it in **Weekly history**.

## Read feedback and update status

In your project page, scroll to **Weekly history** and open an update.

### Status meanings

- **Pending**: submitted, waiting for review.
- **Needs revision**: instructor requests changes; your team should update and resubmit.
- **Approved**: instructor accepted the update (status is locked).

### Comments (discussion)

Open the update and use the **Discussion** area to:

- read instructor comments,
- reply with clarification,
- paste links as follow-up evidence.

## Resubmitting when “Needs revision”

If an update is marked **Needs revision**, you can edit and resubmit it:

1. Open the update card.
2. Click **Edit** under “This week’s goals”.
3. Fix goals (and blockers/progress/links if needed).
4. Click **Resubmit**.

Resubmitting sets the status back to **Pending** for review.

Tip: when resubmitting, include links/evidence that address the instructor’s notes.

## Create a purchase request

Use purchase requests for project-related items (components, materials, subscriptions, etc.).

1. Open your project.
2. In **Purchase requests**, click **New request**.
3. Fill in:
   - **Item** (required)
   - **Quantity**
   - **Cost (total, HKD)**
   - **Link** (optional but recommended)
   - **Why it’s needed** (required; justify impact on project)
4. Click **Submit request**.

After submission:

- Status starts as **Pending**.
- Your instructor will **Approve** or **Reject** and may leave a note.

## Common troubleshooting (students)

### “I signed in but see no projects”

- You must be added to a project team by an admin/instructor.
- Send your exact email (the one you used to sign in) to your instructor/admin.

### “Magic link sign-in failed”

Common causes:

- The link was opened on a different device/browser without your stored email.
  - Re-enter your email and click **Complete sign-in**.
- Your school email system delayed the link.
  - Request a new magic link.

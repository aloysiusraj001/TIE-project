# Student Guide (Project Tracking)

This guide covers what **students** can see and do: **courses & projects**, **weekly updates**, **meetings (agenda/action items + comments)**, and **purchase requests**.

## 1) What you have access to

- You only see **projects you are assigned to**.
- Each project belongs to a **course** (shown at the top of the project page).

## 2) Picking a project

1. Sign in.
2. From your dashboard, click a project card to open the project workspace.

In the project workspace you’ll find:
- Weekly updates (submit + history)
- Meetings (agenda/action items + comments)
- Purchase requests

## 3) Weekly updates (submit + respond to feedback)

### Submit a weekly update

1. Open your project.
2. Click **Submit update**.
3. Fill in goals, progress, blockers, and links.
4. Submit for review.

### Understand update status

- **Pending**: submitted, waiting for review.
- **Needs revision**: instructor wants changes; update and resubmit.
- **Approved**: accepted; treated as locked.

### Comments / discussion

Use the update’s discussion area for clarification, links, and back-and-forth with the instructor.

## 4) Meetings (one thread per meeting lead)

Meetings are organized into **threads** based on who the meeting is with.

### Choose a meeting thread

Use **Meeting thread** dropdown to select who the meeting is with:

- Course instructors
- Any instructor on your team’s **project support** list

### Create a meeting

- **New meeting**: creates a fresh meeting.
- **New (inherit last actions)**: copies the previous meeting’s **action items** into the new meeting’s **agenda**.

Optional:
- Set **Proposed time for new meetings** (date/time). You can also set/clear it later inside the meeting.

### Agenda & action items

- Add items, edit text, remove items.
- Click **Save agenda** and **Save action items** to persist changes.

### Meeting locking (important)

Agenda and action items are **locked** when either:
- The meeting is marked **held**, OR
- A **newer meeting exists** in the same thread (you’re looking at an older meeting)

When locked:
- You can still **read** agenda/action items.
- You can still **post comments** (discussion stays open).

### Comments (back-and-forth)

Comments are **append-only**: each post adds a new comment. This supports ongoing discussion even after agenda/action items are locked.

## 5) Purchase requests (request items for your project)

Purchase requests are for items needed by your team (components, services, subscriptions, etc.).

### Submit a purchase request

1. Open your project.
2. In **Purchase requests**, click **New request**.
3. Provide item, quantity, cost, link (recommended), and justification.

### What happens next

- Requests start as **Pending**.
- A **course instructor/admin** approves or rejects and may leave a note.
- After a decision, the request is **locked**.

## 6) Common issues

### “I signed in but don’t see any projects”

You must be added to a project by an instructor/admin. Send them the exact email you used to sign in.

### “I can’t edit a meeting’s agenda/action items”

That meeting is locked (held or not the newest in the thread). Switch to the latest draft meeting in the dropdown.
